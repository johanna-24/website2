System Features

Employee Portal

Employee portal also known as self-service is a feature in an HR system that empowers employees to access and manage their own personal information without needing to rely on HR to do it for them. Employee's can access the following information:

Personal Information

Employees can access their personal information, such as contact details, emergency contacts, and job history. They can also update this information if it changes, ensuring that HR professionals always have the most up-to-date information on file.

Payroll Information

Employees can view their pay slip, including their earnings, deductions, and tax information. This feature allows employees to verify that they have been paid correctly, and to keep track of their income over time.

Benefits Information

Employees can access information about their benefits, such as leave credits, and leave application status.

Work Schedule Information

Employees can request overtime, new work schedule, and other income or deduction check the status of their time off requests.

Timekeeping

Time and attendance management is a critical feature of an HR system as it helps HR professionals to track employee work hours and manage attendance-related processes more efficiently. Here are some of the key functions of this feature:

Time Tracking Management

This feature enables HR professionals to track employee work hours, including start and end times, breaks, and overtime. Employee's attendance can be captured using biometric systems, rfid/qr code scanner, or mobile/laptop devices.

Location Management

This feature tracks employee's location especially for those on field. IT also prohibits employees from logging if they are not within the 2km radius of the office. This also help enhance safety and security by providing real-time location tracking for employees who work in remote areas.

Time Off Management

HR professionals can also use this feature to manage time off requests, including vacation, sick time, and personal leave. Employees can request time off through the system, and HR professionals can approve or deny requests based on company policies and staffing needs.

Schedule Management

The time and attendance feature can also help HR professionals to manage employee schedules, including shift changes, overtime, and holidays. This information can be used to ensure that staffing levels are appropriate and that employees are being used effectively.

Payroll

Cirquolus Payroll System is designed to automate and manage the process of paying employees. It is used to calculate employee pay, withhold taxes, and other deductions, and generate paychecks or direct deposits. Here are some of the key features and functions of a payroll system:

Accurate Payroll Processing

Cirquolus Payroll System help ensure that employees are paid accurately and on time. It can automatically calculate wages, taxes, and deductions, eliminating errors that can occur with manual calculations.

Time-Saving

Automating payroll processes can save HR professionals a significant amount of time. The system can handle tasks such as calculating 's earnings, generating pay slip, and reports for statutory contributions allowing HR professionals to focus on other important tasks.

Cost Saving

Cirquolus payroll system can be cost-effective in the long run. It can eliminate the need for manual payroll processing, which can be time-consuming and expensive. Additionally, the system can help ensure compliance with tax laws, reducing the risk of costly penalties.

Data Securing

Cirquolus payroll system can help ensure that employee information is kept secure. The system is password protected, and only authorized personnel can access payroll data.

Law Adhering

A payroll system can help ensure that companies comply with tax laws and regulations. The system can automatically calculate tax deductions, generate tax forms, and keep track of payroll records, making it easier for companies to comply with regulations.

Human Resource Information System

Personal Data:

This functionality provides HR departments with a secure and organized method to store and manage a range of employee information, including contact details, employment history, educational qualifications, emergency contacts, and other pertinent personal data.

Documents Repository

This feature establishes a secure and structured repository within the HR system for storing and managing various employee-related documents, such as employment contracts, offer letters, resumes, performance evaluations, training records, certifications, and other relevant documentation

Disciplinary

This feature allows HR professionals to effectively record disciplinary incidents, administer warnings or sanctions, monitor the progress of disciplinary cases, and maintain a comprehensive disciplinary history for each employee.

Statutory Details

This feature facilitates the storage and management of statutory information pertaining to employees, encompassing tax details, social security numbers, identification documents, and other legally mandated data.

Device Registration

This feature empowers HR to oversee and restrict employee devices for enhanced security purposes. It entails the registration and approval process for employees' devices, ensuring compliance with organizational policies and bolstering device management and access control measures.

Branch Movements

This feature enables HR departments to efficiently manage and track employee movements across branches or locations within an organization, encompassing transfers, promotions, relocations, and other related changes. It facilitates accurate record-keeping, effective management of organizational transitions, and provides valuable insights into workforce distribution across different branches.
